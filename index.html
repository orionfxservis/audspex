FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common queries about our services, procedures, and products.

Do I need a hearing test?
If you experience difficulty understanding conversations in noisy rooms, frequently ask others to repeat themselves, or notice a constant ringing in your ears, we highly recommend booking a comprehensive hearing evaluation with our clinical audiologists.
Which hearing aid is right for me?
The ideal model depends on your specific hearing loss profile (determined via audiogram), the anatomy of your ear canal, your lifestyle needs (e.g., active, office-based), and budget. Our experts will guide you through all options during your consultation.
How long does speech therapy take?
Duration varies widely based on the patient's age, the root cause of the disorder, and personal progression. Following an initial speech and language diagnostic session, our specialists will draft a customized plan and estimate a timeline (typically 3 to 6 months of weekly sessions).
Do you treat children?
Yes! AudSpeX has dedicated pediatric specialists offering infant hearing screenings, pediatric play audiometry, speech development assessments, and childhood voice therapies in a friendly, comfortable clinic setting.
Can I book online?
Absolutely. You can fill out the request form below to choose a preferred date and time, and our team will get in touch to confirm your appointment within 2 hours.
Do you provide hearing aid fitting?
Yes, our service includes full custom ear mold impressions, fitting parameters tuning, volume programming using computerized mapping, and a comprehensive guide on maintenance and usage.
